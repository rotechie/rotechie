• About

    Enea Antonicelli was born somewhere in Italy in the winter of 1986. The exact date and circumstances of his birth are unknown since he was immediately placed inside a basket and ousted into a river.

    A young woman noticed the basket one morning while picking berries around the muddy banks of the Tiber. She rescued baby Enea and nursed him using the milk of a badger.

    Years passed as Enea grew into a young man; developing his many talents along the way. He discovered photography while selling boar sausage along the outskirts of Rome. Roughly around the same time, he began to rescue small kittens and other animals he found along his journies.
